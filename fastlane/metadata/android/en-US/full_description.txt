Litiluism (ᛚᛁᛏᛁᛚᚢᛁᛌᛙ) is an interactive rune learning app.

Features:
 * Brief introduction into history of runic writing, comparison of different scripts.
 * Introductory exercises to practice one rune at a time.
 * Full transliteration exercises of runic inscriptions spanning multiple regions and epochs.
 * Recommended external materials in multiple languages.

All content is based on books and materials of acclaimed scholars and information from museums and national and regional cultural organisations.
